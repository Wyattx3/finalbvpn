Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. We are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about what data we collect.

1.2 Usage Data

  • Connection timestamps (when you connect/disconnect)

  • Server locations selected

1.3 What We DO NOT Collect

üîí We have a strict no-logs policy. We do NOT collect:

2. How We Use Your Information

The limited data we collect is used for:

  • Providing and maintaining the VPN service
  • Managing your points/rewards balance

  • Displaying relevant advertisements (via Appodeal/AdMob)

3. Advertising

Our app displays advertisements through Appodeal and Google AdMob to support free VPN service. These advertising partners may collect:

  • Advertising ID

4. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary:

  • Device registration data: Until you uninstall the app or request deletion

  • Points/Rewards data: Retained while your account is active

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • All VPN connections are encrypted using modern protocols
  • Data is stored securely on Firebase/Google Cloud infrastructure
  • Regular security audits and updates

6. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase: For authentication and data storage
  • Appodeal/AdMob: For advertising
  • VPN Servers: Our own infrastructure for VPN connections

Each service has its own privacy policy governing their data practices.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:
